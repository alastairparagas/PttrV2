Mission

At Pttr, we strive to prevent as many strays as possible from being euthanized. Pttr will not only increase adoption rates, but ensure happiness within the new owners so both can lead a healthier mental and physical lifestyle. The Pttr platform was made to provide easy access to strays in local animal shelters and rescue centers. Thus, making adoption a more efficient and simpler option.


Stray animals get the short-end of the leash (MAYBE A GOOD PUN? MAYBE NOT APPROPRIATE). They border the edge of euthanasia everyday they are in a shelter. Most strays have two to four weeks for an adoption to happen before they are euthanized. Pttr aims to decrease the amount of euthanasia.
