A second chance

Pttr connects everyday individuals like you to animal shelters in your local area, who need your support - shelters that often lack the resources to help the troubling number of stray animals out in the streets.

Pttr also empowers shelters to easily manage and track both stray animals currently in their care as well as those that are out in the street.

Best of all, Pttr is free - and always will be.
